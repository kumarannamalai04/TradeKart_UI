Every stock trader on this planet is eagerly waiting to know when the market will go up and when it will come down. Why this particular name is based on these 2 animals, Bull and Bear. What is the specialty of these 2 animals, Bull and Bear. Why can’t it be other animals? an Elephant or a Cat. A Frog or a Tiger. Can we rename bullish - bearish into,
Elephantish and Catish
Frogish and Tigerish
The answer is, No, we cannot rename these terms and we have to use them as it is Bearish and Bullish. It’s named after the attacking style of Bull and Bear.
Bull starts with a lowered head, bulging glowering eyes pouring at the ground a few times before a burst of acceleration brings sharp unyielding horns and a thick skull rushing towards the opponent. Bull strikes upwards with its horns, thus pushing prices higher. When stock prices are rising that indicates a Bull market and
Bullish view indicates that the market will move upside
Attacking style of Bear is exactly opposite to Bull. Standing up on its hind legs allows a bear to get more information from its senses of smell, sight and hearing. Some species of bear are very strong while attacking by standing on legs and push the opponent from top to down with its powerful paws. A bear will swipe down the opponent, thus pushing prices down. A bear market refers to a decline in prices. So,
Bearish view indicates that the market will come down
Some other animals may have similar kinds of attacking styles even though the stock market uses a Bull and a Bear. In financial markets, it's always Bullish and Bearish always.

Whether the market goes Bullish or Bearish, the 2 feelings will always conquer the public.
Aggression and patience are contradictory. These are emotions and we can approach the stock market either with aggression or patience. If you are aggressive you can’t expect the feeling of patience and vice versa. These emotions would separate the trades into two different categories. Intraday traders and Delivery traders.
The power of aggression leads every Intraday trader to make entry to the market and exit from the market on the same day. They keep their positions in the market just for a couple of hours and make exit before the market closing time. The market opening time and closing time are very crucial for Intraday traders. They don’t care about the naysayers talking about next week's market momentum or tomorrow’s market direction. But the challenge for Intraday traders would be their skill and time. They have to have a good skill of trading and being an Intraday trader they get very less time to execute the trades ( Buy or Sell ) which means they must be available during the market hours till they finish their trade. They could show their real aggression during the market hours. Intraday traders can have a good sleep at night because they will finish the trades by the end of the day and least bothered about the next day market.

Patience pays you more. Yes, this sentence is very relevant in the financial market. Investors are reaping the leverage out of the stock market with their patience. They are not aggressive. They are not regular traders in the market rather they take Delivery trades. Delivery traders can hold their positions more than a day. But few questions revolving on their head after they hold the position,

FAQ
1. What is a stock exchange ?
Stock exchange provides a platform for the public to buy and sell the shares.
2. What do you mean by share of a company ?
A share is nothing but the ownership of the company divided into small parts and each part is called a share.
3. Who is a shareholder ?
In the traditional sense, a shareholder is a person who owns shares of the company.
4. Difference between the stock market and share market ?
There is not much difference, let’s move on.
5. What are these terms, entry and exit in stock market ?
Entry and exit are similar like buying and selling activities in stock market.
6. What is the role of a stock market trader ?
Stock traders typically focus on making profits by taking advantage of price fluctuation on individual stocks in the market.
7. Does the stock exchange have an opening time and closing time ?
Yes. Stock exchange will open and close in a proper time.
8. How can we differentiate delivery trade and intraday trade ?
Intraday trade has only one day validity. And if you do a delivery trade you can hold the shares as long as you want.
9. Who are stock market investors ?
Investors will buy the shares and hold the shares for a long time with the expectation of receiving financial returns. Investors do delivery based trades.
10. What is the market direction ?
It shows which direction the market moves in upcoming days or in the immediate future.
